Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the BrightLight AP application and describes how BrightLight AP collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts to BrightLight AP, we access only the permissions necessary to publish content on your behalf. We collect your account identifiers and authentication tokens required for posting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the content publishing service. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Your social media authentication tokens are stored securely and used only for scheduled content publishing.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted form and are never exposed in plain text.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service connects to TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube APIs. Your use of these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies. We only access the minimum permissions required for content posting.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will remove all stored authentication tokens and account data within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use minimal essential cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions about BrightLight AP, contact us at hello@contentloop.de
